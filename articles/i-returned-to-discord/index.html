Why I returned to discord

Yesterday I reactivated my discord account after a lot of my friends started asking me to make a discord account.

Discord is evil

I do not like discord one bit, proprietary garbageware filled with privacy violations that not even facebook would have. It's a horrid platform but sadly has gained a ton of success because of it's unique and comfortable user interface.

How I combat this issue

I still have a matrix and telegram account. I prefer those platforms over discord, and I will never mention anything personal on discord. This way discord cannot collect any useful data on me and my activity.

I made a discord server

To any discord users, you can find me as avitld. I also have a general purpose discord server for GNU/Linux and *BSD hobbyists alike. I'd still strongly prefer you join my telegram server though. discord.gg/9NrKBafEZu
