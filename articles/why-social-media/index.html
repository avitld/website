Why I use social media

Many people have asked me: "If you are a free software activist, then why do you use non-free social media applications". This article is my answer to that. Through my usage of social media I try to encourage people to use free software solutions. When most of the world is using social media, then I can't reach many people without having to use social media myself. While it is quite sad that without social media you have no reach to anybody online, it's something that we will have to deal with as social media is not leaving any time soon.
